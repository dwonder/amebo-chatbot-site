About Amebo

Amebo is a non-profit, volunteer-driven project designed to help Nigerians understand their data rights under the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Our goal is to make data protection simple and accessible for everyone.

Important Disclaimer

Amebo is an AI assistant, not a lawyer. The information provided is for educational purposes only and should not be considered legal advice. For any specific legal issue, please consult with a qualified legal professional.
